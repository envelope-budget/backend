About This Report

This report compares your stored account balances with calculated totals from transactions to identify discrepancies.

  • Stored Balance: The current balance saved in your account record
  • Calculated Balance: The sum of all transactions for this account
  • Balance Discrepancy: The difference between stored and calculated balances
  • Cleared balances: Only include transactions marked as cleared

Discrepancies may occur due to manual balance adjustments, imports, or data synchronization issues.
